Local Attraction

  • Gangnam: Gangnam is a representative commercial area in Korea. It's a collection of world-class fashion and luxury brands. Also, Gangnam is famous as a place where you can enjoy the latest music and entertainment culture in Korea. It is also famous as a filming location for music videos of K-pop stars and a place where fan meetings are held.
  • Insa-dong: Insadong is a place where you can experience traditional Korean culture. Here, you can enjoy traditional Korean crafts, food, tead, and more. Also, you can see many Korean architecture and cultural properties. Especiall, Insa-dong is a really good place to travel on foot. Nearby Insa-dong, you can also travel old Korean palaces such as Gyeongbokgung and Changdeokgung.
