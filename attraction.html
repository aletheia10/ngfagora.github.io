Local Attraction

Welcome to Seoul!

Seoul, capital of Korea is a dynamic metropolis with a rich history.

Local Attraction

  • Insa-dong is a place where you can experience traditional Korean culture. Here, you can enjoy traditional Korean crafts, food, tead, and more. Also, you can see many Korean architecture and cultural properties. Especiall, Insa-dong is a really good place to travel on foot. Nearby Insa-dong, you can also travel old Korean palaces such as Gyeongbokgung and Changdeokgung.
    • We also recommend you to visit Gwangjan Market, a crowded traditional market close to Insa-dong.
  • Han River, also known as Hangang, is a prominent waterway flowing through the middle of Seoul. This river holds cultural, historical, and econimic significance for Korea. It is especially popular for biking or walking trails that run alongside the extensive river. You can also enjoy water activities such as cruise or kayak.
  • N Seoul Tower is a complex cultural space representing Seoul. This is geographically located in the middle of Seoul, so it is a good place to enjoy the beautiful scenary of Seoul. Please note that the observatory is open from 10 a.m. to 11 p.m.
  • Bukhansan is a national park which is popular for both locals and tourists seeking outdoor activities such as hiking and rock climbing. Hikers are rewarded with panoramic views of Seoul and the surrounding areas from the mountaintop. The scenary is especially breathtaking during sunrise and sunset.
  • Lotte World Tower is a skyscraper in Seoul. This is ranked as the tallest building in South Korea and the fifth tallest in the world. You can visit an observation deck called the Seould Sky Observatory on the upper floors. Also, this building is part of the larger Lotte World complex including Lotte World theme park and the Lotte World Mall.
