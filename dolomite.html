Over the mountain

Milky Way at Dolimite, Alps in Northern Italy

A cozy mountain hut is in the corner of the mountain at 2,000 meters above sea level. Surrounded by breathtaking views, it's the perfect place to enjoy some beer and ham while waiting for nightfall. As the sun descends beyond the horizon, the Milky Way stretches vertically across the sky, a celestial highway adorned with countless shimmering stars.
